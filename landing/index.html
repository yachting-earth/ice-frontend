Important

Yachting Earth ICE is not a distress beacon, EPIRB or real-time tracking system.

It is a voyage preparation and emergency information system designed to ensure that the right person has the right information if something unexpected happens.

How it works

  1. Create voyage

  2. Invite crew

  3. Crew complete their own profiles

  4. Emergency contact confirms participation

  5. Activate voyage

  6. Arrive safely

    Skipper verifies arrival. Nothing further happens.

    Grace period expires

    Skipper has not checked in after ETA plus the configured grace period.

  7. Emergency contact receives secure access

  8. Information ready if emergency services must be contacted
